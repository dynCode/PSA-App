CRYSTAL PARK

Crystal Park is a family friendly neighborhood in Benoni, with the N12, R23, R29 and the R51 freeways all connecting you to other business districts, such as Springs, Kempton Park, Germiston, and Heidelberg.

New homes in Crystal Park sell from R519 900 for a 45m² Tuscan style starter home that has 2 bedrooms and 1 bathroom and range up to R699 900 for a 75m² Tuscan style home with 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms.
