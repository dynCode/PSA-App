Password Reset

To reset your password, follow the steps below.

  • Enter your membership number in the field below.
  • Click on the "Continue" button below, to trigger the reset request.
  • An email will be sent to the registered email address on your profile. This email will contain the newly generated temporary password.
  • Once you have received the email. Open the App again and go to the login page and click on "Update Password" to complete the reset process.
